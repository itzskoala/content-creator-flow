### Final Approved Visual Package

#### Approved Concepts:
**Post 1: Relatable Storytelling**

- **Composition + Lighting:** Ambient light highlights coziness and comfort, bowl of premium food emphasized. Wide shot showing entire room setting.

- **Platform Optimization (Instagram/Facebook):** Vertical video clips for Instagram Reels; Carousels of before/after scenarios and testimonials.

**Post 4: Curiosity-Driven Hook**
- **Scene Breakdown:** Kitten playing with empty food bag labeled 'kitten gold,' next to bowl with premium kitten food, symbolizing secret ingredient.

- **Platform Optimization (Instagram/Twitter):** Square and vertical videos; Twitter polls asking about importance of ingredients.
